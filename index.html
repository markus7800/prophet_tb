Longest 6-men checkmates

Longest checkmates for endgames up to 6 pieces extracted from my hobby-built tablebase called Prophet TB.

My plan is to make the tablebase fully open-source and available to Lichess.

Table Legend

  • STM: side-to-move pieces
  • SNTM: side-not-to-move pieces
  • #pieces: number of pieces
  • #pawns: number of pawns
  • DTM: distance-to-mate in plies (= mate in DTM/2+1 moves, no 50-move rule)
  • Checkmate Line: clickable FEN which copies PGN to clipboard
